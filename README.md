Dice Roller for Resident Evil Board Game (Unofficial Fan Project)
<br>
https://lfleming-314.github.io/REDiceRoller/
<br><br>
Lose your dice? Joining in remotely but still want to roll? This tool is here to help.
<br>
Just click the buttons to "roll" 1, 2, or 3 of the attack/defense dice, or the zombie contagion die.
